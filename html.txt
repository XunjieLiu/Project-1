<div class="text-area" id="overview" data-waypoint="overview">
  <div class="contain">
  	
  	<p>The Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering delivers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes, and many of our graduates take further studies at world-leading institutions or start their careers with prestigious employers.  <br></p><p>Our highly qualified staff members have a diverse range of international experience in teaching, research and working with industry, which benefits our students in enhancing their learning experience and employability. We are active in research and business interaction and have funding support from various international, national and local sources, such as the European Union, the National Natural Science Foundation of China and industry. We are keen to continue our work with business and industry to serve society and further develop our expertise.</p>
	<p><a href="departments/academic-departments/computer-science-and-software-engineering/about/overview" class="read-more"><span>Find out more about the Department of Computer Science and Software Engineering</span></a></p>
